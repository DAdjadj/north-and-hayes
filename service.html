How it works

A careful start in five steps

You direct the work. We manage the person. Any concern comes to your team leader, never to you.

01

Tell us how you work

After a short conversation, you complete a brief preferences form: your priorities, your style, and the daily window that suits you.

02

We match, without rushing

We take up to ten business days to source and match the right assistant for you. You are not billed during this period.

03

Your introduction, your way

You meet your assistant and team leader on a short one-to-one call, or entirely in writing if you prefer.

04

Secure access in minutes

A simple guide walks you through granting access and sharing any logins safely through an encrypted vault.

05

We settle you in

For the first two weeks we check in each week. After that we step back, and you reach your team leader whenever you need anything.
