Our pledge

Commitments, not aspirations

We operate to this standard from the first day, and we would rather lose a task than cut a corner on your security.

If anything ever looks wrong, you hear it from us first.

  • We never see or hold your password. Delegated access, or an encrypted vault that fills in without revealing it.
  • Two-factor protection on everything.
  • Your own isolated workspace, never mixed with another client's.
  • Access removed within 24 hours when our work ends.
  • Confidentiality in writing, signed by your assistant by name.
  • Approved channels only: email and secure video calls, never personal chat apps.
  • One named contact, and full honesty if anything ever goes wrong.

Felt safety is the product. It is built more by visible discipline than by any tool.
