Your privacy, in plain terms

Draft, to be confirmed with a professional before launch.

What we collect

When you contact us or become a client, we collect the details you give us, such as your name, email, and what you need help with. As a client, we handle the information required to provide your executive-support service.

How we use it

We use your information only to respond to you and to deliver the service. We do not sell it, and we do not share it outside North & Hayes without your consent, except where required by law.

How we protect it

We work to a written security standard: delegated access rather than shared passwords, encrypted storage, multi-factor authentication, isolated workspaces for each client, and access removed promptly when our work ends.

Your choices

You can ask at any time what information we hold for you, and ask us to return or securely delete it.

Contact

For anything about your privacy, email care@northandhayes.com.
